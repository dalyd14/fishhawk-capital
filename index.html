    Core Values

    BUY RIGHT

    We buy on strong fundamentals, primarily targeting B/C class multifamily apartments in value driven markets. The assets will cash flow Day 1, with the ability to force appreciation throughout the holding period.

    FINANCE RIGHT

    We utilize long-term, fixed rate financing and conservative leverage to minimize risk. This allows us to cash flow throughout the entire holding period while offering consistent returns.

    MANAGE RIGHT

    Our team oversees a local, third-party manager with an intimate knowledge of the market. This allows us to maintain a strong pulse on the operations of each property.

    MISSION

    It is the mission of FishHawk Capital to develop a world class multifamily real estate value add investment platform in the Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, and the Carolinas that will generate superior risk-adjusted returns for its clients and stakeholders.
